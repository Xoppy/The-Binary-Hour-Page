Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 Oct 2025

Overview

This App does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data. It only uses TikTok’s API to publish videos on the owner’s account.

Data Access

OAuth permission is used solely to obtain the access token required to publish content on the owner’s TikTok account.

Third Parties

No analytics, tracking, or third-party SDKs are used.

Data Retention

No personal data is retained. Access tokens are stored only as needed to perform posting and may be revoked at any time.

Your Rights

Since the App has no end users and does not process personal data, typical data subject rights do not apply. For any questions, contact the owner.
